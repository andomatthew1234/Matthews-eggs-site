Project

Hey, I'm Matty. I've been working on this project for a few years now. I got chickens back around 2020 and have been selling them to family friends.

I chose the name 'King Royalty' as it signifies how the chickens are treated. They're treated, unlike other brands, with genuine love and care. They not only have enough space for me to call them 'free range', but over 3x the legal minimum space required for me to call it that! They get fed fresh scraps straight from the kitchen every day, as well as unlimited access to delicious seeds, and clean, chilled water. They have access to go inside or outside, wherever they choose, 24/7.

I mainly started it as a result of what seemed like thousands of excess eggs, but it has eventually turned into an ongoing project with a dedicated website. This website I coded myself, with the help of some templates and AI along the way! Shoutout to David - my cousin - for helping me code it, and if you're interested in that kind of stuff, check out my open source GitHub page @ https://github.com/andomatthew1234/Matthews-eggs-site.

Otherwise, it's up to you to choose the King Royalty way or not. Head to the 'Buy' page to help contribute!
