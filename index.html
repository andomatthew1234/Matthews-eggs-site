Welcome

Welcome to King Royalty

Welcome to King Royalty Free Range Eggs, where our chickens are treated like royalty. We believe that happy hens lay the best eggs, which is why we treat our chickens with the utmost care and respect. Our chickens live in spacious outdoor areas, with plenty of room to forage, dust bathe, and socialize with their fellow hens. We make sure they always have access to fresh water and a well-balanced diet, consisting of grains, seeds, and vegetables. We never use antibiotics or hormones, and we only use natural methods to keep our chickens healthy. Our commitment to the welfare of our chickens not only ensures they have a happy life, but also results in eggs that are rich in flavour, colour, and nutrition. Try our eggs and taste the difference that comes from treating our chickens like royalty.
